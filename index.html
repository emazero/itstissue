    EVENT PROGRAMME

    What is “iT’s tissue”

    “iT’s tissue” - The Italian Technology Experience - is a unique event, a total immersion in Made in Italy production technology for the entire range of tissue products for health and hygiene.

    It will take place on June 21-28 2015 in Lucca, better known as the “Tissue Valley” as the heart of the event, and simultaneously in Reggio Emilia and Lecco.

    “iT’s tissue” is conceived to offer professionals from all over the world, from the tissue sector and others, the opportunity to discover the best “made in Italy” technology for the manufacture, packaging and handling of tissue products such as toilet paper, kitchen rolls, facial tissues, napkins and handkerchiefs, rolls and pleated products for the consumer and professional sectors.

    A week’s journey is scheduled through 12 leading companies in the sector, as they open their doors for live demonstrations of their technology. The 12 firms that make up the Network are: A. Celli; Elettric 80; Fabio Perini; Futura; Gambini; MTC; Omet; PCMC; Pulsar; Recard; TMC; Toscotec.

    Only at “iT’s tissue” can visitors feel, hear and see close up the very best of tissue technology, and at the same time enjoy an exclusive cultural and gastronomic experience in fabulous Italy.“iT’s tissue” is the only event of its kind in the world.

    Network

    Tissue Italy is a Network, an innovative form of cooperation which adapts well to the needs of diverse companies. The arrangement allows them to collaborate on common objectives, without giving up their autonomy. The Network's aim is to organise a unique event for the tissue industry: “iT’s tissue”.

    Mission

    “iT’s Tissue” aims to bring together businesses and operators in the sector and showcase Italian excellence, further publicising its value based on cutting-edge technology, committed and experienced personnel, know-how and innovation.

    Code of Ethics

    To guarantee transparency and honesty towards the entire market, the Network firms have signed a Code of Ethics which outlines the common values that form the basis for the initiative.

    2013 Edition

    The first edition of “iT’s Tissue”, held in Lucca, Bologna, Reggio Emilia and Lecco from 22 to 30 June 2013, was attended by over 700 operators in the sector from 70 different countries worldwide. It marked the launch of an extraordinary international event that engaged tissue industry operators from all over the world.

    The President of the Republic Giorgio Napolitano, in recognition of the innovative formula that took visitors on a journey through the most important paper-making district in Italy, awarded the event a Medal for Excellence.

    Why this event is not to be missed

    In the panorama of international events, “iT’s tissue” is unique in the world.
    In fact, only at “iT’s tissue” can the most innovative technologies for the tissue industry be seen up close and functioning.
