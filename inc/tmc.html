TMC - BOLOGNA

TMC – Tissue Machinery Company SpA is one of the world leading manufacturer company of integrated solutions for primary, secondary and logistics packaging of Tissue (rolls and facial) and Personal Care (baby and adult Diapers, feminine protection, Wet Wipes) products. Established in 1997 in Bologna, the Italian Packaging Valley, TMC SpA provides the world market with integrated, innovative, technologically advanced, flexible, and highly efficient solutions for packaging.

With about 1,400 machines installed worldwide, more than 130 direct employees and about 350 outsourced, an average annual turnover of € 50 million, TMC SpA is capable of providing a complete service from design, project management, manufacturing, installation to after sales service for all its solutions.

The experienced technical staff of TMC, supports its clients throughout the whole process, resulting in tailor-made and turn-key solutions that meets the highest quality standards.

TMC SpA has a strong presence in the global market thanks to an important network of agents, built over its 17 years.
