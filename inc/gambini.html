GAMBINI - BADIA LUCCA

Gambini is a leading manufacturer of tissue converting machinery, located in Lucca, Italy, that provides innovative solutions which bring substantial benefits to our customers in terms of value and finished product quality.

Gambini produces converting lines for both for the Consumer and Professional markets offering the most advanced technologies and innovative solutions to meet the market demands by featuring high safety and design standards, flexibility, and operational excellence.

Thanks to continuous investment in R&D activities, Gambini continues to design innovative solutions that bring real time benefits to its customers and provide a lower environmental impact with a higher level of safety.
