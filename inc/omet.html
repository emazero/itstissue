OMET - LECCO

OMET has been designing and manufacturing printing presses for packaging and tissue converting machines since its foundation back in 1963. OMET Group of Companies (260 employees, 4 production plants, a covered production area of more than 20,000 sqm) is also engaged in the production of systems in motion and wipes. The sole Machines Division turnover is over 50 M€.

The Machines Division is located in Lecco, Italy, in two different plants and has a Technology Center for demonstration of its products to the customers and development and testing of new prototypes and materials.
OMET Company started in the sixties with the far-sighting experience of its founder chairman, Angelo Bartesaghi. The first machines, developed and launched for a market in extreme need for innovation, were mostly dedicated to the assembly of mechanical components and to precision processing. Later on, the first machine for labels was the announcement of the future leadership in the production of printing presses and tissue converting machines.

OMET Srl celebrates in 2013 its 50th Anniversary: fifty years of passion for innovation, strive for growth, ambition and global vision. OMET world presence is guaranteed by three subsidiaries in the world following areas in strong growth like the Far East (OMET SUZHOU in China), Latin America (OMET IBERICA based in Spain) and Central and North America (OMET AMERICAS in the US) and a large number of qualified international agents. Such an organization allows to offer customers a punctual and timely pre- and post-sales service (24/24, 7/7 Call Centre availability), but also to design and propose answers aimed at the specific needs of the different outlet markets.

Tissue Converting

OMET has 50 years' experience in the manufacturing of in-line converting machines for the production of napkins and tissue, non-woven and dry-laid disposable products, and thanks to the continuous hard work of its R&D Department is in pole position for what concerns technology development and suggestion of new converting solutions. Its range includes TV840 – two-line converting machine for high production of printed napkins; TV503 – single-line napkins manufacturing modular machine; AS2600, overlapped (C or Z fold) or semi-continuous (Z-W-L fold) paper towels production machine.
