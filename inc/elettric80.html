ELETTRIC80

Elettric 80 - Reggio Emilia Founded in 1980 and headquartered in Viano, Italy, Elettric 80 is a global provider of end-of-line and warehouse automation solutions, including raw material handling and truck loading, which increase operational profitability and efficiency – put simply Elettric 80 technology helps companies cut cost and move products in a fast and effective manner.

Due to years of experience, Elettric 80 understands the automation and storage handling challenges faced by different businesses and has subsequently developed a systematic approach which is proven and underpinned by continuous investment in research and development. Above all, Elettric 80 values its relationship with its customers. It is committed to offering high value consultancy and supplying solutions which are flexible, durable and provide a good return on investment.

Its proposition is based on Freeway® - a highly flexible and scalable material handling system which utilises palletising robots, Laser Guided Vehicles (LGVs), high speed wrappers (Silkworm) and a pallet control system. The entire process is managed centrally by Elettric 80 state-of-the-art management system Smart Decision Maker (SDM).

Elettric 80 has subsidiaries in the United States, United Kingdom, Sweden, Australia and Poland, and is therefore able to offer customers local expertise coupled with international insight. The group has 395 employees and the annual turnover is 84 million euro (2011).
