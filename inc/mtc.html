MTC - PORCARI LUCCA

MTC is an Italian company born in 1995 from the will and tenacity of a highly skilled group of engineers with many years of experience in the field of tissue paper, whose aim was to revolutionize and rejuvenate the Tissue Converting Machinery market.
The headquarters is located in Porcari near Lucca – Italy, in the heart of the European Tissue Industry.
During only 19 years of activity, the company has been able to build hundreds of machines for customers located in all 5 continents, revolutionizing the market with its technology and innovative solutions, which have assured to all customers high profitability and productivity.

SPECIALIZATION IS THE KEY

High technological and innovative solutions provide customers with finished products high levels of quality. MTC is the harmonious combination of multiple elements: business cleverness doesn’t mean to act according to an abstract and chilly logic, but it primarily consists in establishing accountability relationships and in relying on everybody’s application and cooperation.

“Our mission is to consolidate the technical leadership reached in these years among machinery producers for the Tissue Converting Industry, improving day by day our offer and range of products, keeping as our first and principal aim, the customer satisfaction”.

The After Sales Customer Service represents the added value for MTC company plan. A close-knit team with a great professionalism ready to offer all the necessary technical assistance for installation and start up of machines with enthusiasm and skill.

In addition to the service coming from Italy, MTC can also give assistance from North America, with its sister company named T.E.C. Inc., based in Appleton (WI) and strategically positioned to satisfy most of American customers’ needs.
