TOSCOTEC - LUCCA

Since 1948 TOSCOTEC has specialised in machinery for the tissue and paper industries.
From complete tissue production lines to rebuilds, modernization projects and even single components, the company delivers state-of-the-art tissue making and air drying technology.

Based on its unique experience, gained through decades of designing and building machinery, backed by constant research and development, Toscotec’s technologies are characterized by innovation , energy saving and use of the latest Best Available Technology.

We offer a fully integrated solution for tissue operators including complete stock preparation lines, process solutions for improved performance, upgrades and rebuilds of machine sections and complete plants.

Toscotec designs and develops projects based on customers’ real needs. The 100% acquisition of Milltech and Stori has placed it as the main supplier of turn-key solutions of top technology complete lines from 40 to 260 tpd, including slitter-rewinders, stock preparation plants, hoods, dust extraction and energy recovery systems.
Toscotec’s service team engineers work worldwide to add customer value through tailor made, integrated solutions. They provide all-around technical assistance, expert process consulting, spare parts and training and are perfectly placed to assess the best way to increase process efficiency, improve end product quality and reduce resource consumption to a minimum.
